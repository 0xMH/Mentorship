## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### New Wizards
1. Navigate to your personal [[Wizards/|Grimoire]]
2. Click **"Create New Week"** and enter a week number (e.g., **1**) to start your first week.
3. Complete the tasks and take notes
4. **Add all your questions** to the **"Discussion Points for Next Session"** section in your weekly Notes.md file
5. Share your learnings in [[Shared Docs/README|Shared Docs]]

> **ğŸ“ Why track questions in Discussion Points?**
>
> All questions you add to the "Discussion Points for Next Session" section in your weekly notes are **automatically aggregated** into the [[ğŸ“š Wizards MOC|Wizards Dashboard]] under "Prepare for Next Session."
>
> This means:
> - Your mentor can review **all wizards' questions in one place** before sessions
> - Questions are organized by wizard and week for easy reference
> - Your mentor comes prepared with better answers and examples
> - No question gets forgotten or lost in chat messages
>
> Don't wait for the mentoring session to ask, write questions down as you learn, and they'll automatically appear on your mentor's dashboard.

### Mentors
1. Check [[ğŸ“š Wizards MOC|Wizards Dashboard]] for progress overview

## ğŸ“– How to Use This Vault

### For Wizards (Mentees)

#### Your Grimoire
Your **Grimoire** is your personal dashboard. Open it to see:
- Your progress and current level
- What week you're on
- All the weeks you've completed
- Your learning streak

#### How to Use Each Week

**1. Start a New Week**
- Open your Grimoire
- Click "â• Create New Week"
- Enter the week number when asked

**2. Do the Work**
- **Tasks.md** = What to learn this week (videos, readings, practice)
- **Notes.md** = Your space to take notes however you want

**3. Prepare for Mentor Session**
- Add your questions in the "Discussion Points" section of your Notes
- Write down topics you want to discuss
- Do this as you learn, not last minute!

**That's it!** Each week, repeat these steps.
